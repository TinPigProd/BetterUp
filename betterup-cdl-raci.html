A note on this document

This RACI was designed as a proposed framework, not a definitive one. Role structures, ownership, and team composition at the Center for Daring Leadership may differ from what is represented here. The activities and accountability assignments are informed by prior enterprise delivery experience, reasonable assumptions about how the Center for Daring Leadership engagement operates, and insight from conversation with BetterUp. It is offered as a starting point for conversation, not a finished artifact.

Role definitions
Client Delivery Director
Holds the client relationship. Stewards methodology integrity. Orchestrates internal delivery ecosystem. Owns the executive sponsor connection and the value narrative.
Change Partner
Architects change strategy and cascade communications. Builds manager toolkits, ghostwritten executive messages, and listening loops. Defines what great communications look like for this engagement.
Facilitation Lead
Delivers the Dare to Lead curriculum in the room. Holds the methodology under participant pressure. Provides post-session signal to the delivery team. Owns participant experience quality.
Customer Success
Owns the commercial relationship. Manages renewal and expansion context. Provides sales-to-delivery handoff. Stays informed of delivery health to protect the client relationship.
People Insights
Designs and executes measurement approach. Synthesizes data into value narrative components. Tracks Kirkpatrick levels 1 through 4. Supports the executive business review with evidence.
Implementation / Tech
Configures BetterUp platform for the engagement. Manages SSO, provisioning, and API integration. Executes end-to-end testing. Resolves technical issues that surface during delivery.
Vice President
Builds and grows the practice. Senior presence at strategic enterprise accounts. Onsite with customers for key moments. Consults on methodology and integration decisions. Shapes strategic direction for enterprise growth and human transformation platform.
Executive Director
Builds and grows the practice. Senior presence at strategic enterprise accounts. Onsite with customers for key moments. Consults on methodology and integration decisions. Shapes strategic direction for enterprise growth and human transformation platform.
Executive Sponsor · Client
Owns the investment on the client side. Provides organizational context and strategic intent. Shows up visibly at launch and key moments. Champions the program to their leadership team.
Program Lead · Client
Manages day-to-day logistics on the client side. Coordinates participant scheduling and access. Serves as the operating contact for delivery communications. Escalates issues to the executive sponsor.
